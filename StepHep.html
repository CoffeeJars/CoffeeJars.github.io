Stephen's Hepburn Program

(StepHep for short)

Basics

‘StepHep’ is the fusion of the PPL 6-day style of training with inspiration from vintage strongman Doug Hepburn. This program aims to build up my weak points, and provide a fresh way for me to progress.

The accessory movements provided are tailored towards my goals. They should be changed to better suit your needs.

Use 80% of your 1RM for the heavy lifts.
Use 60% of your 1RM for the volume lifts.
90-120 seconds of rest between sets.

Program

Mon/Fri Tue/Sat Wed/Sun Reps
Hepburn Heavy Lifts
Deadlift Bench Squat 8x2-3
Row Press R. Deadlift 8x2-3
Hepburn Volume Lifts
Deadlift Bench Squat 3x6-8
Row Press R. Deadlift 3x6-8
Accessory Lifts
Chin-ups Dips Cable Crunches 3-5x10
H. Curl Skullcrusher Calf Raises 3x12
Facepull Lat. Raise Leg Raises 3-5x15
