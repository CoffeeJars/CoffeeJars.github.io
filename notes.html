1. Motion (in 1 dimension)

Displacement is a vector. Distance from starting location over some time interval, `Delta t`.

Average velocity is the displacement divided by the change in time. `bar v = (Delta x)/(Delta t)`.

Distance is a scalar. The length of the entire path of movement of an object. Always positive.

Average speed is the total distance divided by the total time.
