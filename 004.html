A Weekly Allowance

A great way to begin to get a handle on your finances is to give yourself an allowance. This will prevent you from spending too much.

Why a weekly allowance? Well, a monthly allowance is often too big-picture, and daily allowances are easy to break without the perspective. A weekly allowance hits the sweet spot.

How much should I give myself? A good place to start is to take your weekly income and subtract your weekly expenses. Unless you have bigger problems, this should be a positive number. 10-50% of this remainder is a good place to start.
