S01E02: The Naked Now
Working through Star Trek TNG. Season one is a bit garbage but here we go. Actually episode two serves a really specific purpose it seems. By making everyone 'drunk' and lowering inhibitions it allows for expedited character development. Exposing character motivations, relationships, and all that allows us to understand them and bond with them faster. It's actually a really good episode. It also manages to connect to the kirk era, something I'm sure older fans appreciated and had great suspense as a astroid hurtled towards the incapacitated ship. I like how the day was saved by conservation of momentum. That's cool.
